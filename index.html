Users & roles

Administrator

Full access - setup, content, publishing, governance, audit and user management.

Content Creator

Generates content and manages the knowledge base; submits work for review. No approvals, publishing or user admin.

Reviewer

Reviews, edits and approves or rejects content and escalated conversations. No publishing or settings.

Compliance Auditor

Read-only oversight of governance, audit logs, reports and channel monitoring. Cannot create, approve or publish.

Access follows least-privilege - each role sees only the screens it needs. Switch the View as selector (top-right) to see the sidebar change live.
